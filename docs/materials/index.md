## Outline

### Framing Materials

- Aluminum
- Plastics
- Steel
- Wood

### Exterior Panels

- Aluminum
- Plastics
    - ABS (acrylonitrile butadiene styrene)
    - PP (polypropylene, Coroplast)
    - PMMA (polymethyl methacrylate, acrylic glass, Plexiglass)
    - PC (polycarbonate)
    - Others
- Wood
    - Plywood
    - MDF
###  Insulation & Windows

- Insulation
    - PIR (polyisocyanurate) foam
    - PS (polystyrene) foam
    - Other insulators
- Windows
    - PMMA (polymethyl methacrylate, acrylic glass, Plexiglass)
    - PC (polycarbonate)
    - Others
- Other insulators
    - Air
    - Vaccuum
